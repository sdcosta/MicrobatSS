microBat in action

The microBat is constructed from simple, cost effective materials. Using Arduino technology a small sonar sensor detects the distance of objects up to 10 meters. Vibrational motors provide varying response based on the distance of the object. The device is battery operated for mobility. Finally a water resistant polymer is used to hold the materials together.
